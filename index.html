Meet the Owner — Ghassan Abou Araj

Founder of Lustron Detailing, focused on quality service and lasting customer relationships.

Ghassan founded Lustron Detailing from a passion for clean, well-maintained vehicles and the satisfaction of seeing a car transformed. What started as a personal interest quickly became a business focused on delivering quality detailing services and building lasting relationships with customers who take pride in their vehicles.

My journey into car detailing began nearly two years ago, and from the very first vehicle I worked on, I knew I had found something I genuinely enjoyed. Seeing the difference a proper detail can make, both in appearance and in how owners feel about their vehicles, motivates me to keep improving my skills and delivering the best results possible.

Today, I specialize in interior detailing, exterior detailing, and paint polishing, helping customers restore their vehicles' appearance and keep them looking their best. Every vehicle is treated with care, attention to detail, and the same level of dedication I would give my own car.
